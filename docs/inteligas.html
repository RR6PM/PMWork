Up Si Vale

Inteligas

The intelligent App to reduce your fuel expenses

  • Find the lowest price
  • Locate your vehicles and track their performance
  • Secure funds on the card

Context

The company was a voucher issuer with one card to help companies manage their fuel expenses.

The App was developed to enrich the value proposition of this product and to adapt the offer to changes in regulation.

It evolved through three years until partnering with Telefonica to connect telemetry.

Two other proofs of concept were made, measuring fuel with sensors at the tank and allowing card-less payment with IOT.

This project spearheaded the digital transformation of the company.

My Work as a PM

As Product Manager, my responsibilities icluded:

  • Leading a talented team of designers and Product Owners, overseeing the development of various applications, including Inteligas
  • Collaborating with the Visa Innovation Center Miami to define a high-level roadmap for the product's development and future trajectory
  • Working closely with designers to facilitate and animate a Design Sprint®, fostering creative and effective problem-solving
  • Engaging in extensive whiteboarding sessions with both designers and engineers to tackle intricate challenges and streamline the development process
  • Conducting comprehensive proofs of concept to ensure the viability and effectiveness of the product's key features and functionalities
  • Successfully managing the go-to-market strategy for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and coordinating the launch of a joint offering with Telefónica
