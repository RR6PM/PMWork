Up Si Vale

Up Business

Control your expenses and ease the life of your employees

Administrator

  • save time, reduce manual verifications
  • understand your expenses with useful reports
  • profit from organized evidence for audits

User

  • request money for your trip in a few clicks
  • save time easily reporting expenses right at time of purchase
  • seamlessly send report through approval chain

Context

Company was a voucher issuer with three main business lines: food vouchers, expense control, incentives-rewards

Solution developed to enrich the value proposition of business expense card

Application was expected to have budget control and expense reporting capabilities

My Work as a PM

  • Created team of designers and Product Owners that managed the development of this and other applications
  • Organized participative-design sessions with different stakeholders
  • White-boarded with designers and engineers to find solutions to key challenges
  • Tested and evolved prototypes until reaching consensus on user journey for MVP
  • Managed tests and debugging during first stages of MVP

Key Learnings

  • Adjusted my understanding of what an MVP should be and strengthened my capacity to prioritize
  • Got my first real-life, hands-on experience with agile development working hand to hand with engineers and designers
  • Strengthened my understanding of Design Thinking and discovered other related frameworks like Design Sprint
  • Discovered the basic principles of UX
  • Fostered collaboration to onboard former detractors of agile methodologies
  • Increased my management skills since the number of direct reports I had grew, I am thankful for their trust and for the opportunity I had to help them improve themselves
