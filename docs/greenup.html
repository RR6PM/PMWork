green'up

Empower people to take up the best environmental habits

The facts

  • 60-70% of emissions are caused by individual actions (United Nations University)
  • People want to take action, 200M only in Europe

The solution

  • green'up will give people visibility on what they can do and motivate them to action
  • The concept: social media platform with gamification
  • Users: individuals, partner companies and client companies

Context

Product designed during HEC Paris Digital Entrepreneurship Certification program, an intensive Product Development program, partnering with e-artsup and école 42

The curriculum was organized in three verticals:

Pre-workshop MOOCs:

Top notch courses taught by HEC Paris professors

  • Marketing Crash Course
  • New Product Development
  • Design Thinking

Masterclasses:

Here we learned from industry experts, including:

  • Startup Founders
  • Product Professionals
  • VC Q&A

4-week immersive workshop

Work through different problematics of product development, including:

Key Learnings

  • Revisited Lean Startup Book: Approached the Lean Startup methodology with a more mature perspective. Key takeaway: Usability is crucial, but understanding and motivating users come first. Emphasizing the need for thorough testing.
  • Learned SEO/SEM Basics: Acquired foundational knowledge of SEO/SEM techniques. Recognized the value of maintaining a company blog for SEO benefits, which proved valuable when contributing to Edenred teams later in my career.
  • Revised Business Plan Concept: Altered my approach to business planning. Shifted from viewing it solely as a persuasive tool to considering it as a cohesive vision of my goals and associated costs. It serves as both a validation mechanism for ideas and a dashboard for monitoring business progress.
  • Adopted New Tools: Gained hands-on experience with tools that later proved beneficial in my professional work:
    • Utilized UX Expressia for mapping customer journeys.
    • Leveraged Miro whiteboard for collaborative brainstorming.
    • Embraced Notion for comprehensive documentation.
